About Tastea

Tastea : A Brand By Life Of Essence

We present to you- TASTEA, a brand by Life of Essence. We source our products from the tea gardens of Darjeeling, Kurseong and Assam. All our products are hand-plucked with no added preservatives and are 100% natural. We claim to offer innumerable health benefits, diabetes control, cardiovascular health, digestion, anxiety and much more. We aim to improve your beverage experience and offer multiple options when choosing a hot or cold brewed drink. No two cups of tea are the same, but we promise an extraordinary experience in both cups.

Tastea Founder

Roshan Agarwala Roshan Agarwala (founder) lives at the foothills of the Himalayas in a small town named Siliguri in West Bengal. He has grown up around tea gardens and has intrinsic knowledge and love for tea. Upon travelling, both domestically and internationally, for different purposes, he found that people only know about the 'Tapri wali chai'. If there is anything very adventurous, they will try green tea. This gave birth to Tastea. Immediately the 'banya (businessman)' in him took control. We started travelling and learning more about growing tea leaves and bringing them to your cup.
